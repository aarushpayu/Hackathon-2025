Technical Details

Complete information about our AI-powered solution

Project Overview

Profit Pirates is an innovative AI solution designed to revolutionize how financial professionals interact with complex financial documents, accounting standards, and regulatory information. Our platform consists of three specialized AI agents, each expertly trained to handle specific domains of financial intelligence.

Technical Architecture

Our solution is built on PayU's proprietary AI platform Toqan, leveraging advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) with the ability to create specialized agents. Each agent is configured with:

  • Custom Knowledge Base: Tailored datasets for each domain
  • Web Interface: User-friendly chat interface for interactions
  • Conversation Management: Stateful conversations with context retention
  • Document Processing: Advanced NLP for document understanding

Agent 1: Agreement Analyzer

Purpose: Analyzes uploaded financial agreements and answers user queries about contract terms, clauses, and obligations.

  • Intelligent clause extraction and summarization
  • Risk identification and highlighting
  • Quick answers to specific contract questions

Agent 2: Ind AS 109 Expert

Purpose: Provides expert guidance on Indian Accounting Standard 109 (Financial Instruments).

  • Comprehensive knowledge of Ind AS 109 requirements
  • Classification and measurement guidance
  • Expected Credit Loss (ECL) methodology explanations
  • Hedge accounting interpretation
  • Practical examples and case studies

Agent 3: RBI Circular Navigator

Purpose: Helps navigate and understand latest Reserve Bank of India circulars and regulatory requirements.

  • Up-to-date database of RBI circulars and notifications
  • Quick regulatory compliance checks
  • Policy interpretation and clarification

Key Benefits

  • Time Savings: Reduce document review time by 70%
  • Accuracy: AI-powered analysis minimizes human error
  • Accessibility: 24/7 access to financial expertise
  • Scalability: Handle multiple queries simultaneously
  • Compliance: Stay updated with latest regulations automatically

Use Cases

Legal & Compliance Teams:

  • Quick contract review and risk assessment
  • Regulatory compliance verification

Finance & Accounting:
