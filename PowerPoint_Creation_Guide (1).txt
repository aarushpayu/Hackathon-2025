# PROFIT PIRATES - POWERPOINT CREATION GUIDE

## Design Specifications

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Green**: #15b363 (RGB: 21, 179, 99)
- **Light Green Background**: #E8F7EF
- **Dark/Black**: #000000
- **White**: #FFFFFF

### Typography
- **Main Headings**: Large, Bold, Sans-serif (Arial or Calibri)
- **Subheadings**: Medium, Bold
- **Body Text**: Regular, Sans-serif
- **Emoji Icons**: Use where indicated (📄, 📊, 🏦, ⚖️, 💼, etc.)

### Layout Style
- Modern, clean design
- Centered content
- Green accent colors
- Icons and emojis for visual interest
- Call-to-action buttons in green

## Additional Design Tips:

1. **Transitions**: Use simple fade or push transitions between slides
2. **Animation**: Subtle animations for bullet points (appear one by one)
3. **Consistency**: Keep the same font family throughout
4. **White Space**: Don't overcrowd slides - use white space effectively
5. **Icons**: Use consistent icon style (outlined or filled, not mixed)
6. **Branding**: Add logo on each slide (top right corner) if available
7. **Footer**: Slide numbers on bottom right (except title slides)

## Font Recommendations:
- **Primary**: Montserrat, Poppins, or Inter (Modern sans-serif)
- **Alternative**: Calibri or Arial (if custom fonts unavailable)
- **Sizes**: 
  - Title: 44pt
  - Subtitle: 32pt
  - Body: 18-24pt
  - Captions: 14-16pt
